My 2019 Summary

Author: Frosty Date: January 27, 2020  21:16:34

First of all I have to say that I am so grateful for all I have. From the breath inside me and good health to the ability to put food on my table everyday. My body is getting healthier and I am learning how to live alone. I understand I have a very long way to go. I began by trying to make up for something missed, like to knowing different places and meet some friends. Whether be it on a trip, study or fitness.

I know that like today in my future there would be true love , there would be betrayal , there would be ups and downs , there would be friendship , success and failure , happiness and sorrow but I am sure there would be difference. And I remember a quote from Mr. Oscar Wilde, “people are afraid of themselves，nowadays，the terror of society，which is the basis of morals,the terror of god，which is the secret of religion - these are the two things that govern us.” Oscar Wilde is effectively saying that I must live my life in a good and true way, if I don’t, then I will destroy any happiness that I can have in my life.

It’s very difficult to listen and follow your heart because there are consequences and I need to conquer that fear. I will stand firm for my choices because this is me since then. Whenever I meet challenges, I think it would help me grow a little bit because it clarifies my passion and what I truly want in my life.
